Frequently Asked Questions

Why is sustainability important to LITTLE?

It is easy to argue that our company is relatively small compared to the ever growing plastic toy manufacturing companies, but LITTLE does not equate to nothing and we believe that small steps can lead to big changes. We continue to strive for sustainability to be a part of those impactful small changes, hence we place much value and importance of our cause.

What makes LITTLE's products sustainable?

Our products are made from fallen or dead trees that are already intended to be burned. Allowing us to operate with minimal environmental impact and with reduced cost and waste.

Where can I buy LITTLE products?

We are proudly affiliated with many fundraising organizations and companies that are working towards the same cause as we are. Check out our Affiliates Page in the Cart icon to find LITTLE Toy Store near you.

Does LITTLE ship internationally?

We proudly say that LITTLE is now available for shipping internationally however we are only able to ship according to the product's availability
